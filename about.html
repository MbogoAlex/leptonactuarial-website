Our Journey

Founded by a team of Kenyan actuarial experts, Lepton Actuarial began with a simple mission: to bridge the gap between traditional actuarial science and emerging sustainability challenges in East Africa. What started as a boutique consultancy has grown into Kenya's leading actuarial firm.

Today, we combine decades of actuarial expertise with cutting-edge machine learning to solve problems others consider intractable. Our work has been recognized by the Insurance Regulatory Authority (IRA) Kenya and featured in leading East African business publications.

"Our commitment is to empower Kenyan businesses with world-class actuarial insights that drive sustainable growth and financial resilience."
